About Us

BREZ - Amanda Berišaj

BREZ brand was founded by designer Amanda Berishaj. BREZ began it’s story with the preservation of tradition through its first collection titled “Across Generations.”

Amanda Berishaj was born in 2002 in Podgorica. She graduated from the Secondary Vocational School "Spasoje Raspopović" as a fashion design technician. She earned her degree in Fashion Design from the University of Donja Gorica in Podgorica and continued her postgraduate studies at the TTF University in Zagreb, focusing on Fashion Design.
