ACROSS GENERATIONS

The "Across Generations" collection represents the connection between tradition and modernism through fashion creations.

This collection is inspired by motives from the Albanian folk costume “xhubleta” , highlighting the importance of preserving tradition and the ways in which it can be passed down from generation to generation.

Our vision is to achieve brand recognition through our innovative creations.

The need for quality clothing. The need for an innovative and different style of clothing as a result, standing out from similar styles.
